What it does for you

🔍Scrapes hiring posts

Searches LinkedIn for your roles with human-like pacing — scroll pauses, random gaps, a daily cap — so it browses the way you would.

🤖AI-ranked leads

Google Gemini extracts role, seniority, location, skills, and how to apply from every post, then the best matches rise to the top.

📬Finds the apply route

Application emails and links are pulled out of the post text, so Tier 1 leads are one click from an application.

📊Live dashboard

Your sheet gets a Dashboard tab with charts — new leads per day, leads by role — plus an Activity log of everything the agent did.

📧Email digests

New actionable leads arrive in your inbox as a clean digest. Nothing is ever sent twice.

🖥️Runs in your browser

A local control panel where every action is a button — log in, scrape, categorize, email, start or stop Autopilot — with live status. No terminal needed.

⚙️Easy to tweak

Edit roles, seniority, email, and keys right on the settings page — no config files, no restarts.

🔒 Your data stays yours. The spreadsheet lives in your Google Drive (the app can only touch files it created — the only permission it asks for). Your LinkedIn session, caches, and profile stay on your machine. Emails are sent from your own Gmail. Your job data is never uploaded anywhere else.

The one optional exception, which you choose: tick “Send anonymous usage stats” and the app sends one ping a day with a random id, the app version, and your OS — nothing more. It's off unless you turn it on, and you can turn it off any time in ⚙ Settings.

Questions

Windows says "Windows protected your PC"

That's SmartScreen flagging an unsigned app (code-signing certificates cost money — this is a free tool). Click More info → Run anyway. Every release is built and packaged automatically by GitHub Actions.

macOS says the app "cannot be verified"

Same story — the app isn't notarized with Apple. Unzip, then right-click (Control-click) JobAgent → Open → Open. On newer macOS you may also need System Settings → Privacy & Security → "Open Anyway" after the first attempt. You can also launch it from Terminal (./JobAgent) — either way it opens its control panel in your browser. Requires an Apple Silicon Mac (M1 or newer, i.e. any Mac from 2020 on).

How do I run it on Linux?

Download, then: tar -xzf JobAgent-linux-x86_64.tar.gz && ./JobAgent. You need Google Chrome installed (the agent drives your real Chrome). Built on Ubuntu 22.04, so it runs on most current distros.

Is it free?

Yes — the app, the Gemini free tier, and Google Sheets all cost nothing for normal daily use. If a day's AI quota runs out mid-batch, the agent backs off and finishes on a later cycle automatically.

Is scraping safe for my LinkedIn account?

The agent mimics a human: randomized pauses, gaps between searches, a daily search cap, and a ~3-hour cycle. Still, it automates your account and LinkedIn's terms don't love automation — keep the default pacing and use your own judgment.

How do I update?

The download button above always serves the newest version. Your profile, sheet, and sessions are files next to the .exe — replacing the .exe keeps everything.
